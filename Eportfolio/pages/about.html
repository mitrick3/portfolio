About

Welcome to my ePortfolio! My name is Trey Fitzpatrick (legally Michael) and I am a junior studying mechanical engineering at Duke University.


I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, and I credit the “techie” culture of the area for cultivating my passion for STEM, which eventually led to me to study engineering at Duke.


I find massive fulfillment in the design and build process, and have enjoyed applying an engineering mindset in traditional applications like building a high-powered rocket with Duke AERO to less direct uses such as financial modeling within the natural gas industry.


I hope to continue developing my engineering skill set during my remaining two years at Duke, and to continue making strides toward a career in which I can apply my hard-earned knowledge.


In my free time I enjoy playing guitar, fly fishing, and mountaineering.
